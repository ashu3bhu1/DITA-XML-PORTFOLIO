How to subtract numbers

Here you will find instructions for using subtraction feature of JPS Calculator

To subtract numbers, you must know what is subtraction is

  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the (-) button
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Click on the (=) button

The result from subtraction is displayed

you want to subtract 2 from 3. First enter the number 3. Click on the (-) button. Enter the number 2. Click on the (=) number to get your answer
